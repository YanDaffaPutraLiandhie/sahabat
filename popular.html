Adolescent reproductive health is an important aspect of overall well-being, as it involves the physical, emotional, and social changes that occur during puberty. During this stage, teenagers begin to experience hormonal changes that affect sexual development, fertility, and body image. Proper education and guidance are essential to help adolescents understand their reproductive system, practice healthy behaviors, and prevent risks such as early pregnancy, sexually transmitted infections, and emotional stress.
